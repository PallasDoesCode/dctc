AUTO COLLISION REPAIR TECHNOLOGY		    
       

 
Instructor: Tim Nance

The Collision Repair Technology Program provides education and training experience in current and emerging technology designed to provide specialized skills and technical knowledge relevant to automotive body repair. Good reading and basic mathematics skills are needed to follow instructions and diagrams in technical manuals with today's computerized diagnostic equipment and technology. This program is NATEF certified.
