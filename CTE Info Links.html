Career Technical Education
 

The mission of DeKalb County Alabama Career and Technical Education is to build students competence in technical skills and in core academic subjects. We provide technical preparation in a specific career field, therefore, leading the student to high-skill, high-wage, and high-demand occupations. This website is designed to assist students and teachers with easy access to internet links, documents, professional development, career opportunities, and much needed career technical information for DeKalb County, Alabama. Integrated Academic/Technical Education

Career Tech instructors increase students' understanding, raise their achievement, and equip them with knowledge and skills needed in today's world. The integration between traditional academic studies and vocational skill is evident in the community as students pursue careers after graduation. With the knowledge available at our schools, students have an increased ability to qualify for skilled jobs for the future.
