Career Library
   
   
 

The Career Library at the DeKalb County Technology Center provides information and resources for teachers, students, and the community. Information linking the secondary with post-secondary careers is available. Safe and Drug-Free information is available at the Career Library. Resources available include books, videos, DVD's, and various media supplies and information.
