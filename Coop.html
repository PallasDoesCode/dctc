Cooperative Education is a structured component of the Career/Technical Education curriculum that integrates classroom instruction and learning with productive, supervised work experiences in field related to students' career objectives. Content is planned for students through a cooperative arrangement between the school and employer using the resources of the community. The program partners students with teachers and employers who have specific responsibilities for providing students with progressive school and work experiences that integrate theory and practice. The program is supervised, directed, and coordinated by the local education agency.
