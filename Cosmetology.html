Cosmetology
     
Instructor: Tracy Martin Assistant: Stacey White
   Email: tlmartin@dekalbk12.org
Cosmetology is the art and science of beautifying and improving the hair, nails, skin, and study of cosmetics and their application. The program provides hands-on-training in a realistic setting allowing students the opportunity to master the skills of hairstyling, hair shaping, coloring, permanent waving, hair relaxing, nail technology, and skin therapy. The education will provide the students with most of the hours needed to obtain a license through the State Board of Cosmetology.
