Annual Fundraiser

As you may already know, each year our students from throughout the county have an annual fundraiser.  We sell a discount card in order to make money to attend the local, state, and national contest. Unlike your local home school, we do not have any other way to make money (like ballgames, PTO, booster clubs, ect…). It is very important that we do well with this fundraiser in order to afford to send our students to compete in state and national contests.  We are experiencing exciting—and measurable—results in the SkillsUSA and HOSA competitions.  We are very proud of the success of our students.  By helping us with a discount, or by purchasing cards, you are ensuring that our students have the opportunity to participate in National Recognized competitions. You will also be providing assistance to each and every community by ensuring that our communities are recognized on a National Level in a positive way.  We know that we really do live in the best place in the world and we want everyone to know it!!!  The end result will be continued acceleration of learning for every student and a stronger community because of it.
