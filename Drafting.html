DRAFTING DESIGN TECHNOLOGY		    
       
 
 


Instructor: Tim Turner

Drafting Technology provides information for students interested in architecture, engineering, and related drafting instruments and related drafting fields. Students develop skills using drafting instruments and gain valuable knowledge using Computer-Aided Drafting (CAD) application. Students acquire visualization, sketching, and CAD drawing skills essential for a wide array of career opportunities in engineering, manufacturing, and construction areas.
