Various Programs Available

There are many programs available at the DeKalb County Technology Center that offer students with the help needed to become high school graduates as well as employable citizens. At DCTC, we believe that every students deserves the same opportunities regardless of personal struggles. We insure that no person shall on the grounds of race, color, disability, sex, religion, creed, national origin, or age be excluded from participation in, or be denied the benefit, or be subject to discrimination in any program, activity, or employment. We go the extra mile to provide for individual students with programs provided at our school. Also we have formed partnerships with colleges to provide students with courses that will serve as college credit after graduation. To access the programs above, just click on the link provided.
