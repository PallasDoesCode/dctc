MASONRY

Training in masonry covers safety, the identification and use of tools, mortar and cement mixing, and brick and block laying. The student will gain a sense of accomplishment and pride as he or she progresses through the steps of building simple structures to more complex ones. Such training comes with assurance of succeeding in the world of work in the masonry field.
