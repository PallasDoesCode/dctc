ACCESS

Distance Learning

Facilitator: Diane Dixson
   
The ACCESS Distance Learning program provided by the state of Alabama provides students with access to quality instruction and coursework by blending rigorous Web-based and Interactive Videoconferencing (IVC) courses taught by Alabama certified and highly qualified teachers. With ACCESS available, students here at DCTC are provided the opportunity to complete academic courses which they need to graduate and also enroll in a CTE course. Without this program many students would not have the opportunity to learn the much needed skills in their career area.
