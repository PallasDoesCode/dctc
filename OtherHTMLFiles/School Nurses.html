About our Nurses...

Chris Pierson RN,BSN, NCSN is the School Nurse Supervisor for the Dekalb County School System.  Mrs. Pierson received her nursing degree from the Ida V. Moffet School of Nursing at Samford University in Birmingham, Alabama.   She became a national certified school nurse in July 2007.  She is an active member of the Alabama School Nurse Association (AASN) and the National School Nurse Association (NASN),  Mrs. Pierson strives to work with the students, parents and the staff of the DeKalb County Board of Education to provide the health services needed in our school system.

At DeKalb County Technology Center, Mrs. April Phillips, RN, is the full-time nurse and is available to provide care when needed. Mrs. Phillips graduated from Northeast Community College with a nursing degree after recieving a business administration degree. She designed and maintains the DeKalb County Technology Center website and the DeKalb County School Nurses website. Mrs. Phillips has been the school nurse since 2006. Mrs. Phillips has been married since 1997, and has two children ( Lindsay and Blake). As the nurse, she enjoys helping the students and working with a great staff.

My name is Julie Needham and I am the Crossville School Nurse.  I serve both Crossville High School and Crossville Elementary.  I graduated from The Universtiy of Alabama in Huntsville in 2006 with a BSN in Nursing and have been a school nurse at Crossville since December 2006.  I also worked at the Ft. Payne Hospital in Labor and Delivery for a short time. Although they seem like two extremes I have enjoyed both tremendously. There are people at both places that have made it worth while to come to work.   I hope to continue working with children in the schools and helping them to be as healthy and happy as possible.

Mrs. Kim Hale, RN, is the nurse at Ider School. She serves both elementary and high school. Kim graduated from Northeast Alabama Community College in 1984. She is married and has 3 children, all boys. Kim really loves her job. Kim says she loves the feeling that she is helping the children at her school.

Andrea Hastings, LPN, is the nurse at Moon Lake. She graduated from Northeast College. Andrea is married and has two daughters, Braelyn and Brooklyn. She loves to be around kids and loves her job as a nurse. She has been a nurse since 2005 and plans to continue for many more.

Mrs. Kelly Bryson, RN, is the Plainview School Nurse. Kelly graduated from Holy Name of Jesus School of Nursing. She is married to Wally Bryson and has 2 kids. Kelly has been a nurse for many years. She worked in the OB department as well as the Health Department. Kelly says "I love working with the kids and helping them when they are sick"

Donna Stroud, Ruhama Junior High School nurse, was born and raised in Mobile, Alabama and is a graduate of Murphy High School and Mobile College ( now Mobile University). She has experience in cardiac and surgical areas of nursing and has been a school nurse since 2007. She has been at Ruhama since 2010. Her hobbies include growing roses, reading, sewing, and hiking in the Smokies with her husband. She enjoys spending time with her grandsons. Donna says that taking care of the children is very rewarding and enjoys working with the wonderful staff at Ruhama.
