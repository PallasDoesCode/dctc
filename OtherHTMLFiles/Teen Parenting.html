TEEN PARENTING

  
     
 
 
Instructor: Sherry Culbert
The Teen Parenting Program works with students who are pregnant, as well as those students with children, to assist them with the goal of graduation. It provides homebound education after the birth of the child until the mother can return to school. It also helps with referrals to other agencies such as Childcare Network Resource. Teen student dads are also enrolled. Books, Videos, and brochures are available on many parenting issues. Counseling is also available if needed.
