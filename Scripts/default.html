Welcome to the

Technology Center
 
DCTC is designed to assist the student with skills and knowledge to graduate from high school with the readiness for employment in a specific occupational field, or for future higher education appropriate to the student's occupational objective.

Integrated Academic/Technical Education

Instructors at DCTC increase students' understanding, raise their achievement, and equip them with knowledge and skills needed in today's world. The integration between traditional academic studies and vocational skill is evident in the community as students pursue careers after graduation. With the knowledge available at our school, students have an increased ability to qualify for skilled jobs for the future.
