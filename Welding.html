WELDING TECHNOLOGY

  
   
 
   
 

Instructor: Jim Higdon
 
   

The Welding Technology Program is designed to provide specialized classroom and laboratory experiences leading to entry level welder status. The program provides instruction in a variety of welding. Processes and provides a comprehensive coverage of current welding practices found in industry. Persons planning careers in welding need manual dexterity, good eyesight, and good hand-eye coordination.
